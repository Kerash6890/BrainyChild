What is Montessori?

The Montessori method is a child-centered approach to education developed by Dr. Maria Montessori that values the human spirit and full development of every child.

Prepared Environment

Carefully designed spaces that support natural development through exploration and discovery.

Unique Materials

Scientifically designed learning materials that isolate concepts and allow for hands-on learning.

Individually Paced

Each child works at their own pace, following their natural curiosity and interests.
